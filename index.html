What is Drift?

Drift is a one stop website which gives you a chance to help improve your mental health. There are so many things you can do! You could explore positive affirmations to improve gratitude, follow a breathing animation to help anxiety, get ideas for small things to boost your specific mood, play games with mindfulness in mind, get a 10 song playlist based on your mood, and get a mindful activity to benefit your emotional well-being based on your interests.
